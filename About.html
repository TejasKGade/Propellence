Our Story

The Propellence Journey: A Decade of Technical Excellence

Propellence was founded in 2015 with a simple but ambitious vision: to become the most trusted recruitment partner for companies building the future of technology. We began our journey in Germany, focusing on what we knew best – helping automotive engineering companies find exceptional leadership and technical talent in an increasingly competitive market.

Our deep understanding of automotive ER&D quickly set us apart. As word of our expertise spread, client demand naturally led us to expand across Europe, establishing strong partnerships in Sweden, France, Italy, and eventually throughout the European region. Each new market taught us valuable lessons about cultural nuances, technical requirements, and the unique challenges of scaling engineering teams across borders.

The period around 2019-2020 marked a pivotal moment in our growth. As Global Capability Centers (GCCs) for engineering R&D companies began gaining significant momentum in India, we recognized an extraordinary opportunity. Our proven track record in European markets positioned us perfectly to become the go-to partner for ER&D GCCs scaling their operations in India. We brought the same level of technical depth and cultural understanding that had made us successful in Europe to this dynamic new market.

The post-COVID era accelerated global digital transformation and remote collaboration, opening doors to even broader opportunities. We began receiving strong interest from North America, Japan, and Australia – markets hungry for the specialized technical expertise we'd been cultivating for years. Today, we proudly serve clients across North America, Europe, India, and APAC, with each market benefiting from the deep domain knowledge and partnership approach we've refined over the past decade.

What started as a boutique firm with big dreams has evolved into a trusted global partner, but our core philosophy remains unchanged: exceptional companies deserve exceptional talent, and both deserve a recruitment partner who truly understands their world.

Geographic Markets


Japan: Precision Engineering Meets Innovation
Japan's engineering excellence demands deep cultural understanding and meticulous attention to detail. We've successfully placed senior technical leaders since 2020 across Tokyo, Osaka, Hamamatsu, Nagoya.

Our Focus: Automotive engineering, smart manufacturing, AI applications. We understand Japan's consensus-driven culture and long-term partnership approach.

Europe: Our Foundation Market
Europe is where we began our journey in 2015, building deep expertise across diverse markets and complex regulatory environments. Today, we serve our clients across Germany, Nordic region, France, Italy, and the Netherlands.

Market Expertise: GDPR compliance, automotive safety standards (ISO 26262), and medical device regulations. We navigate multi-country talent mobility and varying employment regulations seamlessly.

USA: Rapid Growth in Tech Innovation Hubs
We serve both Fortune 500 enterprises and high-growth startups with equal expertise.
Market Presence: Strategic relationships with leading tech companies, automotive innovators, VCs, and industry accelerators. We understand both corporate culture and startup agility requirements.

Focus Areas: Electric vehicles, autonomous driving, AI/ML, semiconductor design, medical devices, and industrial IoT.

India: GCC Excellence and Engineering Talent
India represents our fastest-growing market, leveraging the country's emergence as a global engineering powerhouse. We specialize in Global Capability Centers (GCCs) and have helped international companies establish and scale their Indian operations.

GCC Specialization: Leadership team recruitment, cultural integration support, and knowledge transfer processes.
